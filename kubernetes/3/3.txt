1. in kubernetes the object that exposes your application to the external world is typically a Service of type NodePort.
a NodePort service exposes the app on a specific port. external traffic can reach the NodePort service by accessing the specific port.

2. i created nodeport-service.yaml

kubectl apply -f nodeport-service.yaml

kubectl get svc -n my-namespace #to check if the service works

minikube service my-nodeport-service -n my-namespace --url

(the url i got http://127.0.0.1:52273)
